Curriculum

Faculty Modules

Each module is taught by the Board member who wrote the standard.

Focus: Calculating the financial cost of operational drag.

This module introduces the Friction Index (ƒi), a proprietary formula for converting CX pain points into line-item P&L costs. Participants learn to run a Friction Stress Test — identifying the specific touchpoints that are silently eroding EBITDA.

  • · Mapping friction events to cost centres
  • · Running the four-quadrant kill-switch analysis
  • · Delivering the Friction Audit report to C-Suite

Focus: Converting sentiment metrics into P&L performance.

NPS scores are insolvent metrics. This module replaces them with the Solvency Score (Ts) — a composite index that maps directly to gross margin retention, LTV, and churn probability. Participants learn to build a Solvency Dashboard that CFOs can act on.

  • · The EBITDA-CX correlation model
  • · Building the Solvency Dashboard
  • · Communicating Ts to an Audit Committee

Focus: Auditing automated agents to prevent capital destruction.

Every AI hallucination that reaches a customer is a financial event. This module provides the forensic framework for auditing GenAI agents — establishing guardrails, kill switches, and escalation protocols that protect both the customer and the balance sheet.

  • · Classifying hallucination risk by customer impact
  • · Building the AI Governance Audit trail
  • · Regulatory and fiduciary framing of AI risk

Focus: How to present Solvency Risk to a CEO and secure the mandate.

The final module is a boardroom simulation. Participants learn to compress three modules of forensic data into a 12-slide board deck — then present it under pressure to a panel of IACS faculty acting as a Audit Committee. Graduates leave with a ready-to-deploy Solvency Risk Report template.

  • · Framing CX risk as a fiduciary obligation
  • · The 12-slide Solvency Board Deck
  • · Boardroom simulation & live Q&A protocol
