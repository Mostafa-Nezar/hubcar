Forensic Solvency Audits

“Your dashboard says ‘Green’. Your P&L says ‘Red’. We find the leak.”

Every enterprise has a gap between what customers report and what the balance sheet records. Silent friction — invisible to CSAT, untracked in NPS — is the mechanism by which satisfied customers become churned ones, and healthy margins become destroyed ones. The IACS Forensic Solvency Audit closes that gap with mathematical precision.
