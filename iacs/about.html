About

The Central Bank of Experience

The Institute of Applied CX Science was founded on a single premise:
Customer Experience is a financial discipline, not a marketing one.

Mission

Forensic Integrity.
Institutional Trust.

The Institute of Applied CX Science was established to answer a question that no CX association, consultancy or technology vendor had adequately answered: What is the financial cost of poor customer experience, and how do you calculate it to board-level precision?

The answer required a new science — not sentiment analysis, not journey mapping, but a forensic discipline grounded in P&L mathematics and governance protocol.

As the Central Bank of Experience, we don't just research CX; we define the solvency standards by which enterprise customer bases are audited and valued.

The IACS Principles
I
Integrity of Data

No metric enters a board report unless it can be verified against a financial source.

II
Authority of Science

Every framework is peer-reviewed by the Board before publication or deployment.

III
Accountability of Results

IACS engagements carry contractual solvency performance targets. We are accountable to the P&L.

IV
Clarity of Language

We do not use jargon that cannot be translated into an income statement. Ever.
